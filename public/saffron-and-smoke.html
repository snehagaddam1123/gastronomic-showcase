Our Story

A Decade of Flavor & Fire

Born in a small kitchen with a wood-fired oven and an unshakable belief that food, at its best, is a kind of memory you can taste.

10+Years of Craft
Meet the Chef

Chef Marco Aurelio

Trained in Florence, seasoned in Marrakech, and grounded in his grandmother's kitchen — Marco's cooking is a passport. Every plate at Saffron & Smoke is a love letter to the markets, mentors, and meals that shaped him.

He believes the best ingredient is patience: a 12-hour brisket, a four-day sourdough, a sauce reduced until it whispers.
